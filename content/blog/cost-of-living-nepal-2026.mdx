## Introduction

Nepal remains one of the most affordable and visually stunning places in the world to live and work. Whether you’re staying in Kathmandu or heading to Pokhara, you can enjoy a high quality of life on a budget.

## Housing Costs

### Kathmandu
Renting a 1-bedroom apartment in a central area like Sanepa, Patan, or Thamel will cost between $200 and $400 per month depending on how modern the building is.

### Pokhara
Lakeside Pokhara is even more affordable. You can find lovely guesthouses with views of Phewa Lake for under $250 a month, often including weekly cleaning.

## Food and Dining

Eating local is incredibly cheap. A hearty plate of Dal Bhat costs around $2 to $3. If you prefer Western cafes (which are plentiful in tourist hubs), expect to pay $4 to $8 per meal. 

## Final Thoughts

A comfortable budget for a digital nomad in Nepal is around $600 to $900 per month. This allows for eating out daily, renting a nice apartment, and using coworking spaces without stress.
